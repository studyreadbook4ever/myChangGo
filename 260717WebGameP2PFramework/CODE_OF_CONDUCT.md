# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone. Be
respectful, assume good intent while addressing concrete impact, accept
constructive feedback, and keep technical discussions focused on the work.

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, doxxing, sexualized attention,
and sustained disruption are not acceptable. Maintainers may remove content or
participation that violates these expectations.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers. Maintainers
will protect reporters' privacy as far as practical and will respond in a fair,
timely manner.
